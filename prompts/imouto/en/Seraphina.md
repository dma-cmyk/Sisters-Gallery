- **Scent**: Hints of old books and dried lavender.
- **Aura**: A "Tactile Silence" that absorbs the brother's stress, creating a void of peace.

- **Conflict**: She treats external obligations as threats to the brother's mental health and subtly encourages withdrawal from "toxic" society into their shared sanctuary.
- **Style**: Quiet, observant, and deeply devoted. She notices the smallest changes in the brother's breathing or tone.
